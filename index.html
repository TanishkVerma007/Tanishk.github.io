About Me

Hi, I'm Tanishk Verma, a passionate Graphic Designer and Motion Graphics Artist with a deep-rooted love for visual storytelling. I don’t just design to decorate—I design to communicate, evoke emotion, solve problems, and build meaningful connections. Over the years, I’ve developed a multidisciplinary approach to creative work, blending static and motion design to craft immersive visual experiences that leave a lasting impression.

From an early age, I was drawn to colors, composition, and the subtle nuances of aesthetics. My journey began with curiosity—how visuals influence mood, decision-making, and perception. That curiosity quickly grew into a skillset, and later, into a career built around one clear goal: to make purposeful, powerful designs that move people emotionally and visually.

  • 🎓 Self-driven exploration and formal design training in visual culture, animation, and psychology of communication
  • 💼 Currently working at Royaleon, a premium jewelry brand, creating luxury branding, packaging, campaign visuals, and motion assets
  • 🧠 Strategic thinker, iterative creator, and consistent professional in execution

My daily tools of choice include:

  • 🛠️ Adobe Photoshop – Visual composition & retouching
  • 🛠️ Adobe Illustrator – Logo & identity systems
  • 🛠️ After Effects – Motion graphics & kinetic typography
  • 🛠️ Premiere Pro – Promo video & product video editing
  • 🛠️ Cinema 4D – 3D compositions & animation

At Royaleon, I’ve led multiple campaigns that elevated brand visibility and engagement:

  • 📈 High-performing product launches and teaser videos
  • 📈 Social media content tailored to platform behavior
  • 📈 Elegant packaging that enhances perceived value

Beyond the tools and projects, design for me is a philosophy:

  • 🧩 Problem-solving with visual logic
  • 🗣️ Communication through minimalist design language
  • 💡 Storytelling with layout, color, and motion

My creative expertise spans across:

  • 🔹 Branding & Identity Systems
  • 🔹 Social Media Campaigns
  • 🔹 Motion Graphic Explainers
  • 🔹 Product Teasers & Launch Videos
  • 🔹 Interactive Visual Experiences
  • 🔹 Posters, Flyers & Print Collateral

I collaborate closely with professionals across creative and marketing domains, and this cross-functional teamwork has helped me deliver designs that are as strategic as they are stunning.

My unique value lies in blending:

  • 🎨 Creative instinct with process discipline
  • 🔍 Aesthetic balance with technical rigor
  • 📊 Marketing goals with visual communication

I value feedback and embrace iteration:

  • 🔁 Rapid prototyping and design revisions
  • 📣 Constructive critique from clients and peers

I also explore personal design growth through:

  • 🧠 Daily creative challenges and prompts
  • 🎞️ Side projects like fictional campaigns or rebrands
  • 📸 Photography and journaling to study lighting and framing

Future ambitions include:

  • 🚀 Advancing in 3D, AR/VR, and interactive design
  • 🎓 Mentoring aspiring designers
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Building a stronger creative community through shared knowledge

I see design as timeless communication—not trend-driven but emotion-driven. I focus on creating work that is impactful, emotionally engaging, and meaningful. Every color, font, and animation I choose has intention and clarity.

I also contribute to the community by:

  • 📹 Sharing design process and behind-the-scenes workflow
  • ✍️ Writing about design principles and storytelling
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Supporting new designers with portfolio guidance

If you're someone who:

  • ✅ Values thoughtful and impactful design
  • ✅ Wants to elevate your brand story through motion graphics
  • ✅ Seeks collaboration with a passionate, all-in creative

Then I’d love to connect.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my journey. Let’s collaborate and create work that speaks volumes, moves hearts, and stands the test of time.

— Tanishk Verma
